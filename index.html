About Me

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kyung Hee University. I work in the area of Machine Learning with Prof. Ngo Anh Vien and TaeChoong Chung. I am particularly interested in Reinforcement Learning applied to games and robotics. My research ideas touch the areas of developing deep reinforcement learning algorithms to deal with hierarchical and partially observable tasks. I have recently been focusing on deep multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms dealing with hierarchical and partially observable tasks.

In the past, I completed my bachelor degree in HoChiMinh City University of Technology (Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam) where I worked with Prof. Dang Tran Khanh on developing biometric apps for smartphone security. I spent most of my childhood in the peaceful country Phu Yen and then spent five years of my life at Ho Chi Minh City before moving to Korea to start my Ph.D.
