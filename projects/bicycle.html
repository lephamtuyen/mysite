Controlling Bicycle Using Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient Algorithm
Tuyen P. Le and TaeChoong Chung

Controlling a bicycle without human interaction is still a challenge for researchers. Most of the studies on this topic focus on the physical area of bicycle or designing controllers based on automatic control knowledge such as feedback con- troller, LQR controller. This study focuses on applying a state-of-the-art deep reinforcement learning algorithm called Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient to control the bicycle. The bicycle can use the learned controller (agent) to keep balancing or reach a specified goal
